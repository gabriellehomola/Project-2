Frequently Asked Questions

Option number 2: A list of ten frequently asked questions! By, Gabrielle Homola

Website Questions

How do I log into my account?

You can log into your account by entering your chosen username and password into the selected boxes.

How do I comment on posts that I like?

You can voice your opinion on posts by writing in the designated box usually following the post in question.

Can I interact with others on this Website?

Yes you can by commenting on there posts or becoming involved in what they are interested on the website.

How Does the Search Feature Work?

You can search anything you like by typing your question into the designated search box. Your next step would be to click search and await the results.

The search results will depend and rely on how you asked your question or what you typed into the search box.

User Accounts

Can I have more than one user account on a website?

Nope you are limited to one account.

How Do I change my user profile photo?

Upload a photo of your choosing or use on of the default options that we give to you.

Is there a way I can delete my account with this website if I choose to?

Yes, at any moment you can delete your account.

This website is for your benefit.

Support and Contact

How can I get in touch with Staff Support?

You can get in touch with staff at any moment through email or phone.

Will I be able to delete all my account data?

As mentioned earlier you can delete your account whenever you need to.

All of your personal data will be deleted but there will be a record that you once had an account with this website.

Is there support 24 hours a day?

Yes we are able to answer questions twenty four hours seven days a week.
